How it was filmed 21 Jump Street (2012)

21 Jump Street (2012) is a hilarious action comedy about two undercover cops who go back to high school to bust a drug ring. Jonah Hill and Channing Tatum star as Schmidt and Jenko, two former high school classmates who are now partners on the police force. When they are assigned to go undercover at their old high school, they must relive their teenage years in order to catch the bad guys. The film is full of laughs and action, and it's sure to entertain audiences of all ages.
